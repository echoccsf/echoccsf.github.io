Below are 3 interesting WURFL capabilities and what they do.
Capability Description of Capability
manufacturer_name Identifies the name of the device's manufacturer if the name is different from the brand.
bluetooth_version Identifies the version of the device's supported Bluetooth spec.
mobile_browser_version Identifies the device's browser version.
